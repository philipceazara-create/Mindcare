Coping & Self-Help

Daily Routine & Structure

Keep regular sleep and wake times (7–9 hours).

Plan your day with a balance of tasks, rest, and enjoyable activities.

Stress Management & Relaxation

Breathing exercises: Inhale deeply, hold, exhale slowly.

Creative outlets: drawing, music, or crafts reduce negative emotions.

Healthy Lifestyle

Nutrition: eat balanced meals and drink enough water.

Limit alcohol, excessive caffeine, or drugs that worsen symptoms.

Positive Thinking & Self Talk

Replace negative thoughts with balanced ones.

Celebrate small wins to build confidence.
