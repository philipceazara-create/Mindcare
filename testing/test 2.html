Tips to prevent mental health issues

1

Practice Self-care daily

Take time for rest, proper sleep, healthy eating, and regular exercise. A balanced lifestyle keeps both your body and mind strong.

3

Talk about your feelings

Sharing your thoughts with friends, family, or a counselor helps release stress and prevents emotions from building up inside.

2

Manage Stress in Healthy Ways

Use relaxation techniques like deep breathing, meditation, journaling, or hobbies to handle stress before it becomes overwhelming.

4

Stay Connected and Seek Support

Build supportive relationships and don't hesitate to ask for help when you need it. Strong connections protect your mental well-being.
